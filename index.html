Collaborative jewelry workshops


There just aren’t enough accessible jewelry workshops, that offer an affordable, fun & welcoming experience. Our mission at Rage is to provide access to the skills, tools and techniques of jewelry design to everybody, inspiring a DIY movement in personalizable fine jewelry.

Anyone can be a jewelry designer, and we want to show you how.

A sustainable handmade jewelry line


All of the jewelry created for our capsule collection are handmade to order in our apartment turned workshop. Each piece is made out of sustainably sourced metals and gems, a lot of love and sometimes a little bit of rage.

A little about us


Born out of the frustration of two MFA students  with a love for jewelry and collaborative workshopping, Rage is a passion project that began with a literal bang.

Zina and Shirley are NYC based designers, hoping to create a community based collaboration by inspiring and empower local artists/designers.
